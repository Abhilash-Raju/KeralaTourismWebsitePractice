Kodikuthimala

Also known as the Ooty of Malappuram, Kodikuthimala is situated at an elevation of 520 metres above sea level, at the highest geographical point in the hills of Amminikkadan. The rough topography of the hills makes it a preferred choice for advanced level trekkers. The picturesque location is a paradise for photographers and nature lovers.

About Malappuram

The word Malappuram means "terraced place atop the hills" or simply "hilltop", derived from the general geographical characteristics of the city. Malappuram was a military and administrative headquarters from ancient times, though several of the ancient history of the city is hardly seen recorded.

Nedumkayam Rainforest

Nedumkayam Rainforest in Malappuram district has a magic around it: just step into it and within moments, the scent of virgin forest will take over all your senses. The fresh air and clear water along with a wealth of rare species of flora and fauna, will make you wonder why you didn't come here before.
